Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Built from the ground up for macOS. Fast, private, and beautifully designed.

Live Speed Chart

Animated, real-time download and upload speed graph with a glowing live endpoint. Watch your bandwidth at a glance.

Smart File Selection

Pick exactly which files to download before anything starts. Confirm your selection, then hit Start — no auto-downloads.

File Explorer

All your downloaded files organised by type — Videos, Music, Documents, Images, Archives — in a searchable library.

Auto Light & Dark Mode

Seamlessly follows your macOS system appearance. Switch between dark and light — Bitflow adapts instantly.

Full Torrent Control

Pause, resume, stop and prioritise any torrent from the toolbar or right-click context menu. You're always in control.

First-Launch Setup

A clean onboarding flow lets you choose your download folder before anything starts. Settings persist between sessions.

How it works

Up and running in seconds.

No configuration needed. Install, choose your folder, and go.

1

Install Bitflow

Download the .dmg, drag Bitflow to your Applications folder. Done — no Java, no dependencies.

2

Add a torrent

Paste a magnet link or open a .torrent file. Review and select the files you want, then confirm.

3

Download & manage

Watch live speed charts as files arrive. Pause, prioritise, and browse your library — all from one window.
